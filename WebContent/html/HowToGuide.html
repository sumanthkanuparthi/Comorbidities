A Quick "How To" Guide to the Comorbidities Application

  • Open a patient's entry using the patient's MRN or Encounter ID in First Net.

  • On the left panel in the First Net environment for that patient, click on the

    Comorbidities link.

  • When the lab results for the patient becomes available the comorbidities

    will be displayed as shown below

  • Validate the displayed comorbidities:

    • Hover on comorbidity (see arrow above) and a magnifying glass will

      appear next to it (see figure below).

    • Click on magnifying glass to view the lab result for the selected

      comorbidity as well as its reference ranges.

    • Select a valid comorbidity by clicking the checkbox next to it.

    • Click on "Other Comorbidities" to manually select other

      applicable comorbidities from a drop down list.

  • Use the icon on the top right to generate a report of the selected

    comorbidities. Place a patient sticker on the upper right corner, sign and add

    to patient's chart.

  • Use the "green back arrow" to go back and select different

    comorbidities. To print the PDF report on the printer, click on the "Print Icon"
