Respiratory Failure


Following are the conditions for Respiratory Failure:

  • Acute: If PO2 <= 60 or O2 Sat levels <= 90 on current encounter

  • Chronic: : If (PCO2 or ETCO2 ) > 45 and (PCO2 or ETCO2 ) > 45 on both

    current and sprevious encounters

  • Acute on Chronic: if PO2 <= 60 or O2 Sat levels <= 90 on current encounters

    and (PCO2 or ETCO2) > 45 and (PCO2 or ETCO2) > 45 on both current and

    previous encounter
