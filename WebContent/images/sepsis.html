Sepsis

Following are the conditions for Sepsis:

  • Lactate value > NORMAL OR
  • anionGap > NORMAL_HIGH OR
  • heart rate > 90 and respiratory rate > 20 OR
  • WBCCount > 12 or WBC count < 4 OR
  • increased D Dimer and Blood PH OR
  • Rectal temp < 96 OR
  • Urine output < 300 OR
  • Platelet count or albumin > NORMAL or blood pressure < NORMAL
